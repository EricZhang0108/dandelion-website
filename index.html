We are

The Dandelion Project

A student service club at Dartmouth aimed at helping disadvantaged children in China reach equal education.

Proudly Worked With

"Education is the most powerful weapon which you can use to change the world."

Nelson Mandela

Our Mission

We are a group of Dartmouth students who are interested in bridging the education gap in China, beginning with the Dandelion Middle School.

Weekly Meetings

We hold weekly meetings on campus to spread awareness and work together on projects.

Projects

Our projects aim at helping students with English learning with materials such as videos and games.

Alternative Winter Break

We select a few Dartmouth students each winter break to fund their volunteering trip to China and bring back valuable experiences.
